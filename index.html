About


As a graduate student at Northeastern University, I am currently enrolled in the Master's degree program in Information Systems. My academic pursuits are primarily focused on research and development within the expansive domain of computer science and engineering.
Driven by a profound interest in advancing technology, my current research initiatives center around projects involving machine learning and robotics. Specifically, I am engaged in the exploration of methodologies that integrate machine learning principles with robotics applications.
Within this realm, my work involves the development of robotic systems capable of autonomous learning from their environment. Leveraging advanced techniques such as reinforcement learning and genetic algorithms, I endeavor to construct robots endowed with adaptive capabilities, paving the way for enhanced functionality and performance.
This endeavor represents a fusion of theoretical knowledge and practical implementation, aimed at pushing the boundaries of innovation within the fields of machine learning and robotics. Through rigorous experimentation and analysis, I seek to contribute novel insights and solutions that address real-world challenges and drive technological advancement.
I am committed to the pursuit of excellence in research and development, and I am enthusiastic about the opportunity to collaborate with like-minded individuals and organizations in furthering the frontiers of science and technology.
